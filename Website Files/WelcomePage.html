What is Thundering Lies?

Thundering Lies game that follows the inspiration of the classic Fallout games
in that it is text based with very little in the way of graphics and animation. It instead relies on the player's imagination create a voice and
face for their character and the NPCs that they interact with.

Why should you play Thundering Lies?

Are you looking for a game that you can fully imerse yourself in?
Do you want to actually have to think about you choices when you play?
Do you want your actions to have consequences?
Is your computer not able to handle the insane graphics of modern games?

If you said yes to any of these questions, then try Thundering Lies!
